# Horizon Logo Opener Design

## Style Prompt
Premium architectural reveal for a construction and decoration brand: black studio background, crisp white drafting lines, restrained silver glow, and measured cinematic motion. The logo should feel as if it is being drawn by flowing construction lines before resolving into the original brand mark.

## Colors
- Background: #030404
- Foreground logo: #f7f7f2
- Soft white: #dfe3dd
- Steel glow: #9da8a8
- Deep shadow: #0a0c0c

## Typography
- Use the original logo artwork for all brand text to preserve exact English and Chinese lettering.
- Do not recreate the logo text in HTML.

## What NOT to Do
- No colorful accents or neon palettes.
- No generic gradient hero treatment.
- No replacement fonts for the logo.
- No busy particle field that distracts from the brand mark.
- No jump cuts; motion should feel continuous and architectural.
